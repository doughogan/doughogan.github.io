An Alternate View: strong and em

We know the <b> and <i> tags for bold and italics, but there's another way to achieve the same visual formatting:

  • The <strong> ... </strong> tag pair produces bold text.
  • The <em> ... </em> tag pair produces italicized text.

These tag pairs are more strongly encouraged than the <b> and <i> tags today. The reason is accessibility: screen readers that speak the content of web pages can work with these tags for the visually-impaired, yet they still display the bold and italic text we know in the browser.
