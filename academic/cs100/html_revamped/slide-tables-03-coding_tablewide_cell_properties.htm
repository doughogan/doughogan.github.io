Attributes of <table> for Cells

The <table> tag has some attributes that may be set:

  • cellpadding
  • cellspacing
  • border

See above for details on what they mean and how they're measured.

Example

HTML As displayed in browser 
<table cellpadding="5" cellspacing="1" border="1">
  <tr>
    <td>first</td>
    <td>second</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>third</td>
    <td>fourth</td>
  </tr>
</table>
first second
third fourth
