Width and Height Attributes of the Applet Tag

(This section is intended for students who took CMPSC 221. The last bit probably isn't news to you if you took 221 in Spring 2010 or later. I forgot this in Fall 2009 -- sorry!)

Recall that the height and width attributes of the applet tag can be used to set the size of applets. These attributes are measured in pixels.

General form:

<applet code="path_or_URL" height="# of pixels " width="# of pixels">

Note, however, that they can also be measured in percentages:

<applet code="path_or_URL" height="pct%" width="pct%">
