Advanced Uses of Tables

There are many uses of tables beyond what you might think. Here are just a few ways you can use tables in more advanced ways:

  • Structuring pages: vertical navigation bar in one column, contents in a second (1 row x 2 columns)
  • Formatting
  • Achieving multiple alignments on one line: left in one column, right in another
  • Positioning pictures and keeping contents away from them
  • Nesting tables within tables

Can you think of more?
