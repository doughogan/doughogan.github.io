Top 100, First Half 2020s

I've done decade lists for the last two decades as a big deal to me. I figure I'll rank the first half of this decade, because I can and I want to document this somewhere. There's been a lot of great music, although it's been a rough time more than not.

I thought about ranking the quarter-century. Haven't done that...yet? But that thought is what motivated this...

The yearly charts and this don't match up, and they shouldn't; this factors in how songs age in time, just a shorter amount in this case. Also, given the the Covidian stupidity that was at its height in 2020, I included some older songs I really grew to love in that year's year-end list; it doesn't make sense to include such songs in this one though.

Note that this list is not as well thought-out and orchestrated like the 2010s and 2000s decade-end lists. It's a partial decade, after all. If I do get to a 2020s decade-end, the relative rankings could and would shift.
