[old,recycled, and slightly edited premise of what this is all about...]
a list of some of the best songs i've been listening to this year that haven't been on the radio or anything else like that
the albums list of albums has certainly influenced this list
anything that's in the top 100 of the year or which has ever been on the top 30 has been disqualified
with some random commentary and interesting/bizarre/catchy/why-you-might-know-the-song/whatever lyrics too... 

and i'm aware that
(a) there are plenty of songs on here that weren't REALLY released in 2002, but they're close and this is when i discovered 'em...although, most of 'em are the artist's most current releases
(b) i overdid the lyrics at the beginning and got lazier as time went on...or was it that the songs in the top 10 are just too quotable
(c) the rankings aren't perfect and you could probably convince me that i liked number n a lot better than n+1 and i should rerank 'em, but i'm not going to because it's not supposed to be that serious; it's supposed to be fun...
