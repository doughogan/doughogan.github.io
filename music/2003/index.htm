The Top 100 Singles • The Top 15 Albums • The Best 50 Songs That Weren't On the Radio

Here you have it, my year-end lists for 2003. I suppose I could put a punchline here, but then that'd save you the fun of reading through it all. So... suffice it to say you'll see a lot of Something Corporate here. The piano rock band lands a total of 17 (if I counted correctly) entries in the three lists. Both Yellowcard and Fountains of Wayne show up eight times each. Enjoy the lists! Send comments to djh300 (at) psu.edu.
