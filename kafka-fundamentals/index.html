  • Critical Thinking Challenge Exercises - A set of seven conceptual questions to challenge you to think deeply about the concepts in the presentation (with solutions available)
  • Hands-On Activities - Two activities to experience Kafka Fundamentals firsthand:

Then you will observe partitioning and two different kinds of offsets. There are a few enrichment sections to explore a few more concepts that came up in the Fundamentals course.
